Vision

Building a self-sustained and developed Kebbi State that will be among top 10 states in Nigeria

Mission

Partnering with Kebbi state Government to mobilize the Kebbi indigenes and non-indigenes to unlock the economic, security and socio-political potentials of the state

Objectives

1. To mobilise both the Kebbi elites, youths, artisans, students and the grassroots to unlock Kebbi state potentials
2. To train different classes of Kebbi citizens and empower them with strategic and sustainable support structures to accelerate Kebbi state growth.
