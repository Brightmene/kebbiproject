What we do

Welcome to Our Kebbi Project

This is a new Kebbi State where the successful Kebbi indigenes partner to encourage and empower Kebbi youths to unleash their potentials to transform their lives and communities thus contributing in the greater Kebbi state socio-political, security and economy development. Our Kebbi Project promoters believe that the true treasure of the state lies within its citizens especially the young people of Kebbi state therefore when they are trained, equipped and empowered properly with sustainable support structures they will turn the 21 Local Government Areas and the 35 districts of Kebbi State into sustainable economic hubs. We invite you to Our Kebbi journey to join us in unlocking the economic potentials of the state. Fill the form and the project team will contact you with more details.

Kebbi Arise Conference

Kebbi Arise Conference is annual event that seeks to mobilize strategic indigenes to discuss and set the Kebbi growth Agenda Kebbi Arise Conference is annual event that seeks to mobilize strategic indigenes to discuss and set the Kebbi growth Agenda; the Conference has three basic sessions which includes
Kebbi Speaks: strategic speakers are invited to lead discussion on the potentials of kebbi state and the frameworks to maximize the potentials
Kebbi Arise Plenary: experts are grouped to discuss on specific sectors to come up with a developmental working document for the sector that will be presented to the Kebbi State Government for consideration and factoring into their developmental agenda and Kebbi Arise

Kebbi arise awards

Outstanding Kebbi indigenes and non-indigenous Kebbi lovers are awarded this twin purpose award which include;
1. as reward for their outstanding commitment in making Kebbi great and.
2. as a commitment to supporting the STARTUP KEBBI Agenda.

Start-up Kebbi

Start-up Kebbi is a skill acquisition, trade, business development, incubation and acceleration centre that operates from Kebbi State Capital with localized centres across the 21 local government areas of Kebbi state, equipped with strategic and dynamic programs to assist in the implementation of the KEBBI STATE ACTION PLAN document.
This document is generated from the KEBBI ARISE CONFERENCE every year in partnership with the State Government, Relevant commissions and other related international development partners.
