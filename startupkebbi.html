Start-up Kebbi


Startup Kebbi is a skill acquisition, trade, business development, incubation and acceleration centre that operates from Kebbi State Capital with localized centres across the 21 local government areas of Kebbi state, equipped with strategic and dynamic programs to assist in the implementation of the KEBBI STATE ACTION PLAN document that is generated from the KEBBI ARISE CONFERENCE every year in partnership with the State Government, Relevant commissions and other related international development partners.
Startup Kebbi programs include;

Startup Kebbi Activate:
This is skill acquisition and strategic business training for aspiring and existing traders, startups, businesses and social enterprises across the 21 Local Government Areas of Kebbi state.

Startup Kebbi Fund:
This is a tactically generated fund from successful kebbi indigenes , outstanding organizations from Kebbi State and international development partners to support Kebbi indigenes to start or grow their businesses or trades.

Startup Kebbi Market Access:
This program has developed a systematic partnership structure to support government market access program such as the Kebbi-Lagos Rice Partnership.

Startup Kebbi Mentors Matchmaking:
This program pairs successful kebbi indigenes with up-coming indigenes for direct grooming and mentorship

Startup Kebbi Resource Mapping:
This is online based portal to map out the kebbi resources across the 21 local governments areas to attract investors to the state as well as create a kebbi state database of who is who in Kebbi State.
